Our Urological Services

We offer complete urological care with state-of-the-art technology and internationally trained specialists. Our department handles everything from routine diagnostics to complex surgical interventions.

Diagnostic Procedures

Comprehensive diagnostic workup including cystoscopy, urodynamic studies, imaging, and laboratory analysis to accurately identify urological conditions.

Minimally Invasive Surgeries

Advanced laparoscopic and robotic-assisted procedures offering faster recovery, minimal scarring, and reduced post-operative discomfort.

Kidney Stone Management

Complete stone treatment including ESWL (shock wave lithotripsy), ureteroscopy, PCNL, and preventive metabolic evaluation.

Prostate Health Treatments

Management of BPH, prostatitis, and prostate cancer including TURP, laser ablation, and advanced cancer therapies.

Bladder Control Solutions

Treatment for incontinence, overactive bladder, and neurogenic bladder disorders using medications, behavioral therapy, and surgical options.

Pediatric Urology

Specialized care for children including treatment of congenital anomalies, reflux, enuresis, and hypospadias repair.

Infertility Treatments

Male infertility evaluation and treatment including varicocele repair, sperm retrieval procedures, and hormonal therapy.

Urological Cancer Management

Comprehensive cancer care for kidney, bladder, prostate, and testicular cancers with surgical, medical, and radiation oncology expertise.

Female Urology

Specialized care for female urological conditions including pelvic floor disorders, recurrent UTIs, and urethral disorders.
