# Quick Start: Finding Your App in Meta Developers Portal

## If You Don't Have an App Yet

1. **Go to**: https://developers.facebook.com/
2. **Log in** with your Facebook account
3. **Click "My Apps"**:
   - Top-right dropdown (your profile picture) → "My Apps"
   - OR direct link: https://developers.facebook.com/apps/
4. **Click "Create App"** (green/blue button, usually top-right)
5. **Select "Business"** → Click "Next"
6. **Fill in**:
   - App Name: "Clinic WhatsApp" (or any name)
   - Contact Email: Your email
7. **Click "Create App"**

## If You Already Created an App

1. **Go to**: https://developers.facebook.com/apps/
2. **Look for your app name** in the list
3. **Click on your app name** to open the dashboard

## Finding WhatsApp in Your App

1. **In your app dashboard**, look at the **left sidebar**
2. **Scroll down** to find **"WhatsApp"** (under Products section)
3. **Click "WhatsApp"** → **"API Setup"**
4. You should see your credentials there

## Direct Links (Replace YOUR_APP_ID)

- **App Dashboard**: `https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/`
- **WhatsApp Setup**: `https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/whatsapp-business/`
- **API Setup**: `https://developers.facebook.com/apps/YOUR_APP_ID/whatsapp-business/get-started`

## Still Can't Find It?

### Check These:
- ✅ Are you logged in with the correct Facebook account?
- ✅ Did you complete the app creation process?
- ✅ Is your app type "Business" (not "Consumer")?
- ✅ Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
- ✅ Clear browser cache and refresh

### Alternative Method:
1. Go to: https://business.facebook.com/
2. Go to **Business Settings** → **WhatsApp Accounts**
3. This might show your WhatsApp setup if already configured
