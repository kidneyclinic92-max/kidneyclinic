Our Kidney Transplant Services

As a leading transplant center, we offer complete kidney transplant services from evaluation through lifelong care. Our experienced team has performed thousands of successful transplants with excellent outcomes.

Pre-Transplant Evaluation

Comprehensive medical, surgical, and psychosocial assessment to determine transplant candidacy and optimize patient condition.

Living Donor Assessment

Complete evaluation of potential living donors including medical screening, compatibility testing, and counseling.

Deceased Donor Coordination

Waitlist management, organ allocation coordination, and rapid mobilization when organs become available.

Advanced Surgical Techniques

Minimally invasive and robotic-assisted transplant surgery performed by fellowship-trained transplant surgeons.

Immunosuppression Protocol

Personalized immunosuppression regimens tailored to each patient's risk profile and tolerance.

Rejection Monitoring

Advanced surveillance including protocol biopsies, biomarkers, and rapid intervention for rejection episodes.

Long-term Follow-up Care

Lifelong monitoring and management by dedicated transplant nephrologists ensuring graft longevity.

Complications Management

Expert handling of post-transplant complications including infections, malignancies, and metabolic issues.

International Patient Coordination

Complete support for international patients including visa assistance, accommodation, interpreters, and travel coordination.
