Our Nephrology Services

Our nephrology department provides comprehensive kidney care from early detection through advanced treatment. We specialize in managing complex kidney diseases with a multidisciplinary approach.

Chronic Kidney Disease Management

Complete CKD care including stages 1-5 management, progression monitoring, and lifestyle counseling to preserve kidney function.

Hypertension & Kidney Health

Specialized management of hypertension related to kidney disease, including resistant hypertension and renovascular disorders.

Dialysis Programs

State-of-the-art hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis services with comprehensive training, monitoring, and family support programs.

Kidney Function Testing

Advanced diagnostic capabilities including GFR assessment, proteinuria evaluation, and comprehensive metabolic panels.

Electrolyte & Fluid Balance

Expert management of complex electrolyte disorders, acid-base imbalances, and fluid overload conditions.

Glomerular Disease Treatment

Specialized care for glomerulonephritis, nephrotic syndrome, IgA nephropathy, and other primary kidney diseases.

Kidney Biopsy & Diagnostics

Image-guided kidney biopsies performed by experienced nephrologists with rapid pathology turnaround.

Pre-Transplant Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment for transplant candidacy including medical, surgical, and psychosocial evaluation.

Post-Transplant Follow-up Care

Long-term monitoring of transplant recipients including immunosuppression management and complication prevention.
